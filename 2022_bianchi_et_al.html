A reduced self-consistent set of files needed to reproduce Fig. 6c of the paper is available in ModelDB.
Simulations launched via the "Run" button will reproduce the (non-filtered) somatic membrane potential as in Fig. 5 of the paper. By clicking on the "Reset/Set parameters" button you can change the values of the peak conductances of the Na+ and M-type potassium currents (KM), the KDR currents, and two A-type potassium currents (for proximal and distal dendrites), before running the model simulation.
It is possible to visualize the experimental traces used for the model creation via the "Experimental traces" panel buttons.

NOTE: the online simulator might run with the a NEURON version newer than the one used for the results reported in the paper. Hence, you might see minor differences in the simulated activity (e.g., number of spikes).
